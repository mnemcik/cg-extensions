**Drafting outbound messages in your voice.** When the task involves drafting or sending a message on your behalf — a Slack message, email, Jira comment, PR description, or any other outbound communication — **load [`VOICE.md`](VOICE.md) before writing the draft.** It captures your register per channel, language conventions, greeting/signature patterns, and a final-pass checklist. Skip when only reading or summarizing — load it **only when producing** outbound text. If `VOICE.md` does not exist yet at the workspace root, copy the seeded skeleton at [`templates/VOICE.md`](templates/VOICE.md) to `VOICE.md` and fill it in.
